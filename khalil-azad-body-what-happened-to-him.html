Khalil Azad Body: What Happened To Him? Beaten To Death By The Robbinsdale Police

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Thursday, September 5, 2024

On the cusp of a year since the perplexing and mystifying expiration of Khalil Azad, it has been pronounced that his demise was caused by an inadvertent drowning.

Notwithstanding, the community of Black Lives Matter in Minnesota and his family assert that the Robbinsdale Police savagery must have snuffed out the life of the 24-year-old.

It is purported by the police officers that on the third day of July in the year 2022, during the early hours, they endeavored to halt a DWI traffic stop.

However, the driver refused to halt, prompting a frenzied pursuit. Eventually, the automobile halted at the 3900 block of Lakeland Avenue North as the driver lost control of the vehicle.

Allegedly, the individual riding the vehicle decamped on foot, eluding capture.

Two days later, the lifeless body found on the Crystal River was identified as Khalil Azad, who absconded from the police pursuit.

Mystery Surrounding the Death of Khalil Azad’s Body

The tragic death of Khalil Azad’s body in Crystal Lake last July continues to remain shrouded in mystery. The Robbinsdale Police initially concluded that Azad, aged 24, drowned in the lake, as per the autopsy reports. However, Azad’s family disputes this conclusion, stating that he was a competent swimmer and could not have drowned.

Azad was stopped by the Robbinsdale Police two days before his body was discovered, but he allegedly fled on foot when they attempted to question him. This incident marked the last time anyone saw him alive.

On the day of the discovery, Azad’s body was found floating in the lake, face down. Despite the autopsy results indicating drowning, there are no clear indications of how he ended up in the lake.

Recent developments in the case have pointed towards the possibility of foul play. Newly released photos of Khalil Azad’s body have led some to speculate that the police may have been involved in his death.

Many people in the Minnesota community have voiced their concerns over the lack of clarity surrounding the incident. A vigil was held in Crystal Lake to commemorate Azad’s life and to demand a thorough investigation into his death.

The community is putting pressure on the authorities to take the necessary steps to bring justice to Azad and his family. Until then, the mystery of what happened to Khalil Azad’s body continues to haunt those who knew him.

Did Robbinsdale Police Beat Khalil Azad to Death and Dump his Body in Crystal Lake?

The death of Khalil Azad, a 24-year-old Minnesota man, has sparked outrage and demands for justice after graphic images of his disfigured face were released. His family and activists are claiming that the Robbinsdale Police may have beaten him to death and then attempted to cover it up by making it seem like an accidental drowning in Crystal Lake.

The Controversy Surrounding Khalil Azad’s Death

On March 1, Khalil Azad’s body was found in Crystal Lake, Minnesota. However, his family immediately raised questions about the circumstances surrounding his death. Graphic images of Azad’s face, showing signs of brutality, further fueled their concerns.

The family claims that the Robbinsdale Police may have beaten Azad to death and then dumped his body in the lake to cover it up. They are calling for a full investigation into the incident, including the release of bodycam and dashcam footage from the officers and medical examiners involved.

Graphic Images Reveal Signs of Brutality

The images of Khalil Azad’s face are difficult to look at. They show scratches, K-9 bites, and dreadlocks torn from his scalp. His family says that these injuries are consistent with police brutality and not consistent with accidental drowning.

The disfigured nature of Azad’s body and face has also raised questions from Black Lives Matter Minnesota. They released a statement claiming that the medical examiners did not account for the extent of the injuries, and that an independent investigation is necessary.

Black Lives Matter Minnesota Raises Questions

The death of Khalil Azad has brought renewed attention to the issue of police brutality against people of color. Black Lives Matter Minnesota has called for an outside investigation into the conduct of the Robbinsdale Police, the medical examiner’s office, and the suspension of all K-9 units in the state.

This incident has also reminded many of past cases of police brutality, such as the deaths of Rodney King, Emmett Till, and Tyre Nichols. The calls for justice in Khalil Azad’s case are a reminder that the fight against police brutality is ongoing.

Demands for an Independent Investigation

The demands for an independent investigation are growing. Minnesota teen activists have joined the call for justice, demanding transparency and accountability from those involved in the case.

The family of Khalil Azad has also called for a full investigation, including an independent autopsy. They are seeking answers to the questions surrounding his death and are hopeful that the release of body camera footage will shed light on what really happened.

Robbinsdale Police Chief Responds

On March 3, Robbinsdale Police Chief Patrick Foley responded to the growing controversy. He stated that he supports an independent review for transparency and that the department will gather and release body camera footage next week.

While this is a step in the right direction, many are still calling for a more thorough and independent investigation. The death of Khalil Azad has sparked a nationwide conversation about police brutality and the need for change. The fight for justice continues.

Calls for Justice and Accountability
