General Hospital Spoilers: Kelly Thiebaud Returns to a Dream Role Shares Fun Restart with Fans

Posted by Martina Birk on Thursday, August 29, 2024

General Hospital (GH) spoilers reveal that Kelly Thiebaud (Britt Westbourne) has been going through some life changes lately, but she’s taking a positive approach and viewing this as a chance to reinvent herself.

Thiebaud recently shared some exciting news with fans on X, the social media platform formerly known as Twitter.

That’s where Thiebaud explained that she was getting back into an activity from her past. It turns out Thiebaud is returning to the dream role of softball player since it’s something she did in her younger years.

“So, part of my restart, reinvention of self, is joining a coed softball league that plays weekly in Central Park. Doesn’t that sound like a dream!” Thiebaud said in her post on April 12.

Thiebaud also offered details on her first official game and shared how long it’s been since she played.

Although Thiebaud might be a little rusty, she’s clearly eager to see if she’s still got all the right moves.

“Tomorrow is my first game and we have a doubleheader! I haven’t played since I was like 14 but I remember being pretty great,” Thiebaud added.

We have no doubt that Thiebaud will indeed be great, especially since this little softball adventure is probably less about winning and more about tackling a nice challenge.

As long as Thiebaud is having fun and doing something that fits with her reinvention plan, it’ll be a success story!

It’s awesome to see Thiebaud making big plans and throwing herself headfirst into the action.

Of course, many GH viewers are holding out hope that Kelly Thiebaud might return to GH as Britt Westbourne or even a brand-new character someday.

It doesn’t look like there are any plans for that right now, but you never know what might happen down the road.

In the meantime, Thiebaud has other things to focus on – like this coed softball league and any other undertakings that might come along.

As for what’s ahead in Port Charles, our predictions point to Carly Spencer (Laura Wright) having a big reaction to Jason Morgan’s (Steve Burton) upcoming Metro Court gift.

General Hospital spoilers say Jason has moved on from Britt for now, so stay tuned for updates on his next chapter.
