Bold &amp; Beautiful spoilers for week of December - The Bold and the Beautiful

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Sunday, August 4, 2024

Bold & Beautiful spoilers for week of December 18:

Monday, December 18:

This could be the beginning of a fantabulous rivalry as R.J. and Zende argue over their grandfather and the company that he co-founded.

Ridge second-guesses himself about the harrowing decision that he made for Eric.

Tuesday, December 19:

Zende is incredulous when a position that he wanted goes to… R.J.

Brooke reflects on her unique connection with Eric.

Wednesday, December 20:

Finn admits his greatest fear to Bridget-that it will be that he will lose Steffy if Eric doesn’t pull through.

Zende makes a startling realization and shares it with Luna.

Thursday, December 21:

With Eric’s life hanging in the balance, Steffy worries that she made the wrong decision.

Finn and Bridget present an option that puts Ridge in an untenable situation.

Friday, December 22:

As Eric lies unconscious and unable to breathe on his own, Steffy, Ridge, Brooke, Finn and Bridget discuss his wishes.

At the same time, Eric’s family lovingly recalls special times that they had with their patriarch.
