The Rise of an Icon

Sandra Bernhard’s journey from Flint, Michigan, to the sparkling lights of Hollywood is nothing short of cinematic.

Her razor-sharp wit and insightful commentary on American celebrity culture and political figures set her apart in a male-dominated industry.

Breaking Boundaries

Sandra’s breakthrough came with her role as Masha in The King of Comedy, for which she received critical acclaim and a National Society of Film Critics Award for Best Supporting Actress.
