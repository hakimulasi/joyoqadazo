Gilmore Girls star Alexis Bledel marries Vincent Kartheiser in secret wedding

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Sunday, August 11, 2024

    Alexis Bledel and Vincent Kartheiser have tied the knot.The former star and the actor said "I do" earlier this summer in a top-secret California wedding, according to .The pair are said to have met in 2012 when Alexis made a guest appearance on Vincent's series, playing his character's love interest.
