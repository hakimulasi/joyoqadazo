Here's What Khloe Kardashian Keeps in Her Nightstand

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Sunday, September 8, 2024

For the sweetest dreams! Khloé Kardashian revealed exactly what she keeps in her nightstand, and there are couple of things you wouldn’t expect from the self-described organizer extraordinaire.

The Keeping Up With the Kardashians star, 32, wrote via her app, “I wanted to share a pretty intimate part of my life with my app subscribers: the essentials I must have in my bedside table! Some of the stuff is expected (hand cream and reading glasses), but other items (like my Hot Tamales) are essential, too! Read on to see exactly what’s in my nightstand.”

PHOTOS: Khloé Kardashian’s Body Evolution 

So what did we discover? Like many people, Kardashian keeps her reading glasses, a notebook (purple and personalized with her name, natch) and a Bible in the drawer beside her bed. Although, probably unlike you, the Strong Looks Better Naked author has a bejeweled Bible, complete with a rhinestone-embellished cross. (She shared a similar one via Celebrate Faith that retails for $265.)

PHOTOS: Celebs’ Favorite Beauty Products

And then, of course, there are the less expected items: the aforementioned Hot Tamales, a box of strawberry licorice, a lighter and a measuring tape — you know, for those late-night moments when you’re considering redecorating your whole room, we assume. (Hey, we’ve been there!)

Kardashian’s nightstand also boasts a few beauty essentials. She uses L’Occitane Lavender Roll-On Relaxing Essential Oil (“to help me relax and fall asleep”) and Nubian Heritage Mango Butter Hand Cream (“[it] quenches my dry skin”).
