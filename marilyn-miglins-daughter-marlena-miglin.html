Who is Marilyn Miglin's daughter Marlena Miglin?

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Tuesday, July 9, 2024

Marilyn Miglin was a successful American entrepreneur, inventor, and veteran television broadcaster.

Miglin was born in Pilsen, Czech Republic, as Marilyn Klecka in 1938.

She is also well-known for her charitable efforts, for which she has received several medals and prizes.

Marilyn has also achieved success as an inventor, developing over 36 scents and perfumes for Marilyn Miglin Cosmetics.

She rose to prominence after her husband, American real estate entrepreneur Lee Miglin, was killed in 1997 by serial killer Andrew Cunanan.

She died from complications of a stroke on March 15, 2022,  at the age of 83 years.

Who is Marilyn Miglin’s daughter Marlena Miglin?

Marlena Miglin was the only daughter of Marilyn.

She got her through her marriage with first husband Lee Miglin in 1959.

Per some publications, she worked as a dancer at Chicago’s legendary Chez Paree nightclub.

She was also a member of former Chicago Mayor Richard M. Daley’s tourist committee, the Illinois Board of Economic Development, and the Oak Street commercial area.
