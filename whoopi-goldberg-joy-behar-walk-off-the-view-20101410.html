Whoopi Goldberg, Joy Behar Walk Off The View

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Saturday, August 17, 2024

Whoopi Goldberg and Joy Behar walked off the set of ABC's The View Thursday after a fiery debate with guest Bill O'Reilly over the mosque near Ground Zero.

O'Reilly, 61, set the two off when he declared Muslims "killed us on 9/11" (see video above).
