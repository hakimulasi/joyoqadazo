Ash By Elegance On Jordynne Grace: I Truly Admire Her Determination And Drive

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Sunday, August 4, 2024

Jordynne Grace is one of the pillars of TNA Wrestling. About a month ago, Big Mama Pump defeated Naomi to become the new TNA Knockouts Champion. Recently, Ash By Elegance shared her thoughts on the top star of the Knockouts Division.

The former WWE Superstar was interviewed by Distortion Media. During the interview, Ash By Elegance shared her thoughts on Big Mama Pump. The former WWE 24/7 Champion mentioned that Jordynne Grace is one of the phenomenal talents in TNA Wrestling.

She also claimed that both Grace and her came from similar backgrounds who used to focus on bodybuilding. Elegance also admires Grace’s passion and determination to be the top wrestler in the division.

“She really is [a phenomenal performer and an inspiration]. I sat with her about a week ago and I really got to talk with her. And I said, ‘Woman to woman, I am very proud of you.’ We come from similar backgrounds. You know, being in the bodybuilding and fitness industry. I know how hard it is. And I really, truly admire her determination and drive. Because I have seen her transformation and is not easy.

“You start second guessing and questioning in your head like, ‘Why am I doing this? Is this the right decision for me?’ And it is very demanding on your body. Just from the bodybuilding aspect. Let alone having to die it down but also wrestle is so hard, so so hard. So hat’s off to her. But she has a phenomenal look and a phenomenal build. And she’s worked so so hard for it. So, Jordynne is definitely definitely an amazing person outside and in the ring as well too,” she said.
