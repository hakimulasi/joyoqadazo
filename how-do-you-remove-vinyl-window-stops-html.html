How do you remove vinyl window stops?

Posted by Martina Birk on Thursday, July 18, 2024
Spread the love

Answer

Vinyl windows are almost always equipped with stops, and the stops are typically simple to remove. Using a flat-head screwdriver, pry upward from the bottom of the window frame, beginning at one end and working your way up to the top.
