La La Anthony: What's In My Bag?

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Friday, July 19, 2024

Aw! La La Anthony and Carmelo Anthony’s 8-year-old son, Kiyan, is adorable. While opening up her Givenchy bag for Us Weekly, the Power actress shared a sweet note her son had written to her on the back of his school photo.

“I cleaned this out before I came today,” she admitted to Us before digging into her matte black purse. “This could either be really great or really terrifying.”

Among the items Kim Kardashian’s 35-year-old pal carries with her? “I always have my acting script because I’m always going over my lines and practicing,” Anthony, who portrays Lakeisha on the Starz series Power, tells Us.

“I have a kid so I like to take pictures,” she adds of her Sony Cyber-shot DSC RX1000 camera. “The city has so much character. There’s so much to see and do and it’s always unexpected things happening, but this camera is so cool because you can plug it into your phone and it uploads directly to your iPhone,” she shares.

Inside her Hermes wallet, the Think Like a Man star carries a photograph of her son when he was first born, as well as his latest school photo. “He wrote on the back: ‘To the best, best, best, best Mommy ever. I love you so, so, so, so, so, so much.’”
