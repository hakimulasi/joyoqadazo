I Stay Away From Drama And Respect Davidos Space Amanda, 2nd Baby Mama Of Artist Says

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Tuesday, July 30, 2024

The second baby mama of Nigeria’s popular Davido, Amanda has disclosed how she co-parents Davido when it comes to their daughter.

Amanda is Davido’s second baby mama who is based in Atlanta, USA. They have a beautiful daughter named Hailey Adeleke. Amongst the three baby mamas Davido has, Amanda is the quietest and drama free.

READ ALSO: Uche Ogbodo Disagrees With Gideon Okeke’s Claims That Female Actresses Are Paid Better Than Male Actors

In a question and answers session on Instagram, Amanda answered a few questions her fans and followers asked her. One of the questions asked by a fan was how she was able to co-parent Davido without any drama like the other dramatic scenes with the other baby mamas.

According to Amanda, there has been no issue between herself and Davido when it comes to catering for their child because they both respect each other’s space and she also tries to avoid drama.

Another fan asked if her daughter Hailey and the daughter of Sophia Momodu, Imade are close and bonding as sisters. Amanda disclosed that the two sisters are very close and love each other.
