Jennifer Lawrences full-frontal nude scene stuns Netflix viewers

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, September 11, 2024

Jennifer Lawrence’s latest film has finally hit streaming, opening it up to a host of new viewers who were stunned to see her now-infamous full-frontal nude scene.

The Oscar-winning actress, 33, stars in No Hard Feelings, an MA15+ rom-com which hit cinemas back in July.

The favourably-reviewed film, co-starring Andrew Barth Feldman, has now dropped on Netflix in the US, where it’s gained further attention for the raunchy scene in question.

In the sequence, Lawrence’s character Maddie is seen emerging from the ocean after going skinny dipping, where she gets in a physical fight with a bunch of teens who stole her clothes.

There’s punches, slaps, high kicks, the whole lot, all while the Hunger Games star is butt naked.

Viewers have agreed it’s an unexpected sight from a decorated Hollywood drama actress.

At the time of its release, many questioned whether a stunt double was responsible for the handiwork, but Lawrence set the record straight.

More Coverage

“Everyone in my life and my team is doing the right thing and going, ‘Are you sure? Are you sure? Are you sure?’” Lawrence told Variety of her decision to do her own naked stunts.

“I didn’t even have a second thought. It was hilarious to me.”

After taking some time off to raise her first child, son Cy, in 2022, Lawrence hard launched her film career with No Hard Feelings, which marked a rare foray into comedy for the drama star.
